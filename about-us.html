Our Story

A Journey Built on Trust & Excellence

DC Group officially began on 1st August 2025, but its story started in 2012 when Mr. Pravin Chaudhari opened a small travel office with a dream to make travel simple and memorable. Over time, he created four specialized companies — DC Tours & Travels, DC Insurance, DC The Travel Expert, and DC Holidays — each serving a unique purpose.

In 2025, his son, Mr. Darvin Chaudhari, brought them together under one brand, DC Group. Today, the group serves 50,000+ travelers, agents, and corporations across India and beyond, turning a humble vision into a trusted legacy.
